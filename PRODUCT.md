# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Intellectuals with curiosity about their own psychology and the psychology of others. They seek deep, evidence-based understanding—not quick tips or personality labels. They're often self-directed learners, educators, or professionals who value rigor and precision.

## Product Purpose

Psyche Map is an interactive encyclopedia of psychology structured around six domains (Personality, Cognition, Motivation, Relationships, Emotion, Self & Identity). Instead of forcing all theories into one visual mold, the app renders each domain's core structure honestly—as hierarchies, 2D fields, timelines, or systems diagrams. Users navigate between domains, drill into concepts through an inspector panel, and encounter evidence quality transparently (cornerstone research, supported, or in revision). Success means a user understands *why* theories have different shapes and *how* they connect.

## Brand Personality

**Credible · Rigorous · Succinct**

The voice is scholarly but warm, never pretentious. Credible means every claim is evidence-backed and caveated honestly. Rigorous means concepts are presented in their true structural form—no oversimplification for the sake of design polish. Succinct means no waste: every word and visual element earns its place. The emotional goal is confidence—learners should feel they're reading the real thing.

## Anti-references

- **Pop psychology.** Avoid reductive personality systems (the "4 types," "color energy," "celebrity diagnoses"). This is not entertainment.
- **Overly playful educational apps.** No rainbow gradients, cute mascots, or gamification mechanics that trivialize psychology.
- **Generic SaaS dashboards.** Avoid the dark-mode tech aesthetic, hero metrics, or identical card grids.
- **Clickbait-y content design.** No emotional manipulation or "you won't believe" framing.

## Design Principles

**Priority order (conflicts decided top-down):**

1. **Structural Honesty** — Render each theory in the visual grammar that reflects its actual architecture (hierarchies as grids, fields as 2D planes, processes as timelines). Never force different structures into the same mold.

2. **Evidence Transparency** — Evidence quality is always visible: distinguish cornerstone research, broadly supported findings, and theories in revision. Never bury or obscure uncertainty.

3. **Progressive Disclosure** — Reveal only what's needed at each moment. Use the inspector panel to keep the initial canvas uncluttered while making the full system accessible.

4. **Parsimony as Restraint** — Curation is the design work. Add only what materially changes understanding. Resist decoration and unnecessary visual layers.

5. **No Valence in Visual Weight** — Neuroticism gets the same visual treatment as Openness; Anxiety gets the same prominence as Joy. The design never editorializes or privileges certain constructs.

6. **Lens Not Label** — Theories are lenses through which to understand continuous experience, not boxes users belong to. Avoid language that reifies or limits.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG AA minimum** — All interactive elements must be keyboard-navigable and properly labeled. Color must never be the only means of communication.
- **Reduced motion support** — All transitions and animations must respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- **Color-blind safe** — The palette is tested for deuteranopia, protanopia, and tritanopia. Do not rely on red/green contrast alone.
- **i18n-ready** — Content structure and component spacing must accommodate languages with different character widths and text direction.
