# Research Prompt v1

Use this prompt when generating or updating psychology landscape research for Psyche.

## Prompt

Map the psychology literature for a consumer-facing psychology literacy app. Prioritize theories that are empirically robust, easy to explain, and useful in everyday adult life. Separate core theories from popular but weakly supported ideas. Summarize the best domain structure, the most useful cornerstone theories, the main exclusions or caveats, and any implications for product architecture or content sequencing.

## Output requirements

- Keep the structure oriented to product decisions, not literature history.
- Prefer broad domains and connectors over niche subfields.
- Call out replication-risk areas clearly.
- End with actionable recommendations for app structure and content priorities.
